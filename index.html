Auto triggers

Your reactions are the button.

Three detection modes — use one or all at once.

Voice shout

Sustained yell above threshold triggers the clip. Tunable sensitivity — works even with headset mic.

Double clap

Two sharp peaks within 600ms. Detects attack + silence + attack pattern to prevent false positives.

Rage mouse

Raw input delta over 50ms window. Detects the instinctive wrist-slam, not normal fast movement.

Features

Everything a clip tool should be.

Built for competitive players, streamers, and content creators.

GPU-accelerated capture

DXGI Desktop Duplication + NVENC/AMF/QSV. Falls back to GDI on older cards. Zero encoding overhead on your game.

Webcam overlay (PiP)

Face-cam burned into the clip at export time — configurable position and size. Separate mic track with ducking.

Perfect A/V sync

WASAPI loopback for system audio. aresample=async=1000 corrects drift across long sessions.

Rolling buffer up to 90s

Premium records up to 90 seconds of pre-buffer. Never miss the setup before the moment.

Instant MP4 export

Clips are trimmed from the buffer and re-encoded in seconds. faststart flag for instant streaming.

Works on any game

No game integration, no overlays, no APIs. Captures at the OS level — any game, any engine.

Privacy

Your clips stay on your PC. Always.

While Medal.tv and Outplayed upload your footage to their servers, Rage Replay doesn't touch the internet.

Zero data collection. No account. No cloud.

Rage Replay is a local Windows app. It does not upload clips, screenshots, or gameplay data anywhere. Your mic and desktop are only accessed for recording — nothing leaves your machine. License validation is the only outbound request (a single key check on launch). No telemetry. No analytics. No background uploads.
